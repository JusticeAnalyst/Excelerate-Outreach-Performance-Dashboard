# Excelerate-Outreach-Performance-Dashboard
### Response, Conversion, and Engagement Analysis  by SLU 0112 – DVT Team 19

##  Project Overview

This repository contains the outreach performance analysis conducted for **Excelerate**, aimed at improving enrollment outcomes from outreach campaigns by at least **15%**. The project includes an exploratory data analysis (EDA) report, a Power BI dashboard, and a written insights report.

The analysis evaluates outreach effectiveness, identifies engagement patterns, highlights data quality gaps, and provides actionable recommendations based on **37,881 outreach records**.

##  Key Insights

- **Low connection rates are the primary barrier** — unsuccessful calls occur at more than 6× the rate of successful connections.
- **Evening outreach outperforms** — despite lower volume, evening calls achieve the highest connection rates compared to afternoon and morning periods.
- **First contact attempts are the most effective** — connection rates decline steadily with each subsequent attempt.
- **Outreach volume ≠ better outcomes** — outreach increased 6× from 2023 to 2024, yet non-connection rates remained consistently high.
- **Performance is geographically concentrated** — India has the highest volume but only average connectivity (29.82%), while Ghana (33.21%) and Nigeria (30.66%) show stronger reachability.
- **Caller workload is heavily skewed** — 3 callers (Rudra, Prajwal, Jyoti) account for over 70% of all outreach activity.
- **Data quality gaps** — invalid and null country entries affect conversion analysis reliability.

##  Recommendations

1. **Shift focus from volume to connection quality** — prioritise reachability over raw call volume.
2. **Optimise outreach timing** — concentrate efforts in evening hours; reduce low-yield morning and early-afternoon slots.
3. **Strengthen contact data quality** — validate and clean applicant contact information to reduce wasted attempts.
4. **Adopt country-specific strategies** — scale efforts in high-connectivity markets; redesign approaches for underperforming regions.
5. **Improve post-connection funnel conversion** — enhance follow-up and engagement strategies to reduce drop-off between outreach and enrollment.
